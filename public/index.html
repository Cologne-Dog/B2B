World's Safest

Sustainable and responsible manufacturing.

The biochemistry of live animals provided all the keys for the decisions we made in our approach. Benign fatty acids are molecules that animals store in abundance. The molecules are characterized as long carbon chain molecules with low volatility and little or no electrical imbalance, known as polarity in organic chemistry.

In contrast, solvents are short carbon chain molecules that are highly volatile, often driven by significant electrical imbalances within their structures. The longer the carbon chain, the more buffer it provides for the electrical imbalances of its constituent atoms. Since the short chain molecules lack this buffer, their volatility is high, thus, causing undesirable effects to live tissues that include the formation of cancers.

The difference between these two classes (short-carbon chain molecules with high volatility and long-carbon chain molecules with low volatility) can be detected by their combustability. While fatty acids are not combustable, solvents are.

The benefits of eliminating solvents extends beyond protection against their damages to sensitive live tissues. Solvents also damage the pesticidal and healing potentials of essential oils and other chemicals. Our system preserves the anti-microbial, anti-fungal, anti-parasitic, anti-viral, and healing potentials of essential oils and other chemicals. Our method can be purposed as the world's safest oil and water emulsions for:

  • General Purpose Deodorizers

  • Topical Delivery System

Non-irritating, non-toxic, hypoallergenic.

Solvents are traditionally known to mix oil and water to produce single layer products, as is often the case for fragrances. However, this comes at a cost. Environmentally, solvent manufacturing has been identified as the leading polluting process in all industrial societies (). Solvent use is regulated by the EPA and OSHA, among others, and solvents are known toxins that damage live tissues and cause cancer.

Ergonomically, solvents are not tolerated by a significant proportion of the human population, and the entire population of non-human mammals. For this reason, solvent-based fragrances are often banned from the workplace and professional settings, such as hospitals and clinics.

The Cologne.Dog™ approach addresses these pain points in fragrance manufacturing. Our oil and water emulsions maintain the strong aromatic properties of oils, while not causing surfaces to become oily. Indeed, Cologne.Dog™ emulsions are hydrating and do not trap dirt or cause grime.
